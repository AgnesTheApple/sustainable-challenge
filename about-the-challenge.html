About The Challenge

As you understood, the Challenge's aim was to trigger sustainable actions by adding in the fun factor.

The first step was to complete a start questionnaire that gave me an idea of what was my daily sustainable actions and in which field I could improve.

Then, for 2 week challenge, I had to keep track of my daily sustainable actions in an activity sheet, and send it to my friend at given times.

We also add a forum on What's App where we could share our daily actions with others players!

Because YES, it is a challenge, so we were severals participants didvided into 3 teams:

  • ITALIAN team (my team!)
  • NETHERLANDS team
  • AUSTRIA/GERMANY team

The Challenge was divided into 3 parts:

  • Trial Period or Individual Challenge. Just 2 days to get familiar with the challenge...just to warm up!
  • Country Challenge, or how things start to be serious! All participants of each team were "competing" against each other! In other words we were all acting as sustainable as possible to get a maximum of points!
  • World Challenge Game on! It was time to show our sustainable best, using all actions that we were applying and sharing since a week. 50 participants, from differents countries, were, during those for 4 days , getting creative and acting more and more sustainable!
