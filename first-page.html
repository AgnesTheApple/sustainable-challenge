My experience as sustainable challenger...

True sustainability is when everyone, everywhere can meet their basic needs forever...

Hello,

My name is Agnès, and today I would like to share with you my experience taking part to a very special challenge!

Today, more than ever, sustainable behaviour is necessary to protect our planet. We can not avoid to see the reality and don't feel concern...

But our lifestyle, our environment, our habbits make it sometimes difficult to act sustainable. "I will do it tomorrow, why i am going to take the bus when all my friends have one car each, vegan food is too expensive...", those are the kind of thought that most of us had experimented or heard one day. I did!

That's why a group of student of the Master’s programme Environmental Management at Lund University decided to lunch the Sustainable Challenge! A way to trigger sustainable actions by adding in the fun factor...

Starting is often the hardest part, but once you get into, it can only get better!
